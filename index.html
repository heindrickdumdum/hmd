About Me

Turning designs into Shopify stores that convert

I'm a Front-End Developer based in Cebu City, Philippines, with 11 years of experience building for the web — the last 7 focused almost entirely on the Shopify ecosystem.

I specialize in translating Figma mockups into pixel-perfect, performant Shopify 2.0 stores using Liquid, custom sections, Metafields, Metaobjects, and modern JavaScript frameworks. I've shipped stores for clients in the US, UK, Netherlands, Germany, Australia, and the Philippines.

Beyond Shopify, I'm comfortable in Vue.js / Nuxt, BigCommerce Handlebars, and full front-end stacks. I stay sharp by actively leveraging AI tools to accelerate workflows and deliver more with less turnaround time.
